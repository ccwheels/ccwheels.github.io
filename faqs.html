Cost & Pricing

How much does it cost to paint a house?

The cost to paint a house varies based on several factors:

  • Exterior painting: $3,000 - $8,000 for an average 1,500-2,500 sq ft home
  • Interior painting: $2,500 - $6,000 for an average home
  • Per square foot: $2.50 - $4.50 for exterior, $2.00 - $4.00 for interior

Factors that affect cost include square footage, number of stories, surface condition, paint quality, and prep work needed. We provide free estimates to give you an accurate quote for your specific project.

What's the average cost per square foot?

Average painting costs per square foot in Phoenix, AZ:

  • Exterior: $2.50 - $4.50 per sq ft
  • Interior: $2.00 - $4.00 per sq ft
  • Ceilings: $1.50 - $3.00 per sq ft
  • Trim/Doors: $3.00 - $6.00 per sq ft

These are general ranges. Actual costs depend on surface condition, paint quality, and complexity of the project.

What factors affect the cost of painting?

Several factors influence painting costs:

  • Square footage: Larger homes cost more
  • Number of stories: Multi-story homes require more equipment and time
  • Surface condition: Extensive prep work (scraping, sanding, repairs) increases cost
  • Paint quality: Premium paints cost more but last longer
  • Accessibility: Hard-to-reach areas may require special equipment
  • Trim and detail work: Ornate trim, shutters, and decorative elements add time
  • Color changes: Dramatic color changes may require multiple coats

Process & Timeline

How long does it take to paint a house?

Timeline depends on project size and complexity:

  • Exterior (average home): 3-7 days
  • Interior (average home): 5-10 days
  • Single room: 1-2 days
  • Whole house interior: 7-14 days

Weather conditions can affect exterior painting timelines. We provide daily updates and stick to our "Finish On Time or We Pay You" guarantee.

What's included in your painting process?

Our complete painting process includes:

  • Preparation: Cleaning, scraping, sanding, caulking, and repairs
  • Protection: Covering furniture, floors, and landscaping
  • Priming: Where needed for proper paint adhesion
  • Painting: High-quality application with brushes, rollers, and sprayers
  • Cleanup: Complete cleanup and removal of all materials
  • Walkthrough: Final inspection with you to ensure satisfaction

Do I need to move my furniture?

For interior painting, we'll move furniture to the center of the room and cover it with protective materials. You don't need to move everything out, but if you prefer to move small items yourself, that's fine. We handle all heavy furniture and ensure everything is properly protected.

Will you provide daily updates?

Yes! We provide daily video updates so you can see progress even when you're not home. You'll receive updates via text showing what was completed each day. This is part of our commitment to transparent communication.

Paint & Materials

What type of paint do you use?

We use high-quality, professional-grade paints from trusted brands like Sherwin-Williams, Benjamin Moore, and Behr. The specific paint depends on your project:

  • Exterior: Acrylic latex paints designed for durability and weather resistance
  • Interior: Low-VOC or zero-VOC paints for better air quality
  • Bathrooms/Kitchens: Moisture-resistant paints with mold inhibitors

We'll recommend the best paint for your specific needs and budget.

How many coats of paint do you apply?

Typically, we apply two coats of paint for optimal coverage and durability. However, the number of coats depends on:

  • Color change (dramatic changes may need more coats)
  • Surface condition
  • Paint quality and coverage
  • Your specific preferences

We'll discuss this during your estimate and ensure you're satisfied with the coverage.

Do you provide the paint and materials?

Yes, we provide all paint and materials as part of our service. This includes paint, primer, brushes, rollers, drop cloths, tape, and all other necessary supplies. You don't need to purchase anything—we handle it all and ensure we're using the right materials for your project.
