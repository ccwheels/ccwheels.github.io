Arizona Exterior Painting Cost Ranges

Exterior painting in Arizona usually costs $2.50–$4.50 per square foot. Stucco, elastomeric coatings, and Arizona sun-resistant paints can push the higher end. Prep, repairs, and trim add to the total.

Why Arizona Exterior Painting Costs What It Does

Phoenix heat and monsoon weather require durable coatings. We use elastomeric and premium acrylic paints designed for stucco in the Southwest. Proper prep—cleaning, caulking, crack repair—keeps the finish lasting longer.
