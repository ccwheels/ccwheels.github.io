5 Signs It's Time to Repaint Your Home's Exterior

Fading, peeling, cracks, and when to schedule a repaint.

1. Fading or Chalky Paint

Sun and heat cause paint to fade or turn chalky. If your color looks washed out or powdery, it’s time for a repaint.

2. Peeling or Bubbling

Peeling or bubbling usually means moisture or poor adhesion. Repainting with proper prep addresses the cause.

3. Visible Cracks

Cracks in stucco or paint should be repaired before repainting. We handle crack repair as part of our process.

4. Mold or Mildew

Mold or mildew needs to be cleaned and treated. We clean and prep before painting.

5. It's Been 8–10 Years

Most exterior paint jobs last 8–10 years in Phoenix. If it’s been that long, consider a repaint.
