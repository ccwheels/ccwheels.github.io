Stucco Painting Cost in Phoenix

Stucco painting in Phoenix typically runs $2.50–$4.50 per square foot. Price depends on surface condition, crack repair, and whether we use elastomeric or standard acrylic paint.

Our Stucco Painting Process

We pressure-wash, repair cracks, caulk joints, prime as needed, and apply elastomeric or premium acrylic paint. Products are chosen for Arizona’s heat and UV exposure.

Why Elastomeric Paint for Phoenix Stucco

Elastomeric paint resists cracking and moisture better than standard paint. It’s a good fit for Phoenix stucco homes and can last longer with proper prep and application.
