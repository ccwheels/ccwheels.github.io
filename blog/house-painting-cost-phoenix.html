Average House Painting Cost in Phoenix

House painting cost in Phoenix varies by project type. Exterior painting typically runs $2.50–$4.50 per square foot, while interior painting is $2.00–$4.00 per square foot. For a 2,000 sq ft home, that’s roughly $5,000–$9,000 for exterior and $4,000–$8,000 for interior.

What Affects House Painting Cost?

Square footage, number of stories, surface condition, and paint quality drive the price. Stucco repairs, trim, and color changes can add cost. We provide free, no-obligation estimates for Phoenix homes.
