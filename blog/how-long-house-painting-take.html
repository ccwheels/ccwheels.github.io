How Long Does House Painting Take?

Typical timelines for interior, exterior, and whole-home painting.

Exterior Painting Timeline

Exterior painting for an average home usually takes 3–7 days. Weather and surface condition can affect the schedule.

Interior Painting Timeline

Interior work typically takes 5–10 days for an average home. A single room is 1–2 days; a full interior 7–14 days.

What Affects the Timeline?

Square footage, number of coats, prep needed, and weather (for exterior) all affect duration. We give a timeline at your estimate.
