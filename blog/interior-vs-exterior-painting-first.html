Interior vs Exterior Painting: What to Do First?

How to plan when tackling both interior and exterior painting.

Start with Exterior Painting

Exterior work depends on weather, so schedule it during favorable months. Interior painting can be done anytime and isn’t affected by rain or heat.

Our Interior and Exterior Teams

We use separate crews for interior painting and exterior painting so you get specialists for each type of project.

Planning Both Projects

Book exterior first, then interior. We can help sequence projects and provide timelines for each phase.
